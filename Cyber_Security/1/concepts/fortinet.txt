**Fortinet is a global cybersecurity company that provides advanced network security solutions such as firewalls, secure SD-WAN, endpoint protection, and AI-driven threat intelligence. It is widely used by enterprises worldwide to protect data, applications, and users across cloud, on-premises, and hybrid environments.**

## 🛡️ Key Products & Solutions
Fortinet offers a broad portfolio of **50+ enterprise-grade products**, integrated under its **Security Fabric** platform:

- **Next-Generation Firewalls (FortiGate):** High-performance firewalls with deep packet inspection and SSL inspection.  
- **Secure SD-WAN:** Combines networking with uncompromised security for distributed enterprises.  
- **FortiSASE (Cloud Security):** Protects remote and hybrid workforces with secure internet and private access.  
- **Zero Trust Network Access (ZTNA):** Enforces strict access control for applications.  
- **FortiGuard Labs:** Provides real-time AI-powered threat intelligence, analyzing **100+ billion events daily**.  
- **Endpoint & Email Security:** Protects devices and communications from malware and phishing.  
- **FortiWeb (Web Application Firewall):** Shields web apps and APIs from known and zero-day threats  [Fortinet](https://www.fortinet.com/products).
