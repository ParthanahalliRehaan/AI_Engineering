Initially, we ran into issues with **localhost crashing** due to certain libraries. To address this, we explored two solutions:

For **static pages**, Render was sufficient (alternatively, **Netlify** works well too). However, when working with Cloudflare, we needed to use **Cloudflare Pages**, which is their dedicated service for hosting static sites.
